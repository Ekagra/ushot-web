Ushot Support

Everything you need to know to start taking dares.

What is Ushot?
Ushot is a social platform where you can dare your friends, accept challenges, and share photo proof.
How do I complete a dare?
When you accept a dare, you'll need to upload a photo as proof of completion. Once uploaded, the person who dared you (the daremaker) can view your proof and approve it with a like!
What are Tags (#)?
Tags group similar dares together. You can follow specific tags (like #peekaboo or #welcome) to see what others are doing and find new dares to accept.
How do invites work?
You can generate a system invite link to bring your friends onto Ushot. When they sign up using your code, they'll immediately receive their first dare from you.
I saw something inappropriate. What do I do?
Safety is our top priority. You can report any dare, photo, or user directly in the app. Just tap the Report button on the post. Our moderation system will review the content immediately.
Why is a photo blurred out?
Some tags, like the #peekaboo tag, use a blur effect by default. Only the specific users who have completed the prerequisite dares can see the clear image.
