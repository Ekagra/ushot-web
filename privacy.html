Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

We build Ushot to help you dare your friends and share fun moments. We only collect the data necessary to make the app work (like your photos for proofs). We secure your data on our cloud, and we don't sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

To make Ushot work seamlessly, we ask for specific permissions and collect certain data:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email, username, phone number, and display name.
  • Camera & Photos: Ushot is built on photo proofs. We require camera and gallery access so you can capture and upload images to complete dares.
  • App Activity: We store the dares you create, accept, complete, and the likes/comments you leave to build your social feed.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used entirely to power the Ushot experience:

  • To create and manage your profile and authenticate your account securely.
  • To deliver notifications when someone dares you, completes your dare, or likes your proof.
  • To process images through our moderation systems to ensure community safety.
  • To run our backend operations efficiently and securely on our cloud servers.

3. How We Store & Protect Data

Security is non-negotiable. Your data is stored on industry-standard, secure cloud infrastructure. We utilize secure tokens for authentication and encrypt storage data in transit. We ensure your account details and uploaded media are guarded against unauthorized access.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data to data brokers or third-party advertisers. Your information is only shared in the following ways:

  • With the Ushot Community: Photos, dares, and tags you post publicly are visible to other users based on the app's mechanics.
  • With Service Providers: We use trusted third-party cloud and database providers to host the app and process data on our behalf.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law, we may disclose information to comply with legal processes or to protect the safety of our users.

5. Your Control & Rights

You own your experience on Ushot:

  • Content Removal: You can delete any dare or content you've uploaded at any time.
  • Permissions: You can revoke access to your Camera, Gallery, or Contacts at any time through your device's operating system settings.
  • Account Deletion: You have the right to permanently delete your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about how your data is handled, or want to revoke your account kindly reach out to us at support@ushot.com.
